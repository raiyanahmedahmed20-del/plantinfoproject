Lemon

About the Lemon Tree

Scientific Name: Citrus limon

Common Names: Lemon, Citrus Lemon

Family: Rutaceae

Native Region: Asia

Plant Type: Small evergreen tree

Fruit Color: Bright yellow

Uses: Fruit consumption, beverages, cooking, medicine, skincare

Details: Lemon is a popular citrus fruit known for its sour taste and refreshing aroma. It is widely used in cooking, juices, desserts, and beverages around the world.

Lemons are an excellent source of vitamin C, antioxidants, and minerals that help boost immunity and improve overall health. Lemon juice is often used as a natural remedy for colds and digestion problems.

The lemon tree grows well in warm climates with plenty of sunlight. It produces fragrant white flowers and bright yellow fruits throughout the year under suitable conditions.

Besides food and medicine, lemons are also used in cleaning products, perfumes, and skincare because of their fresh fragrance and antibacterial properties.
